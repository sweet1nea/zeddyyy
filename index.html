My Birthday Letter to You

July 21, 2025
Hi love,

Happy birthday.

I know I say that every year, pero this one feels different. You're 20 now. That’s not just any birthday. You're not a teenager anymore. You’re stepping into real adulthood, and I just want to talk to you. Not just as your girlfriend, but as someone who’s seen your ugali, your struggles, your growth, and everything in between.

We've been through so much. Sobrang dami na nating pinag-awayan. Ang daming nasabi, ang daming nasaktan, ang daming napagod. But despite all of that, I’m still here. Still choosing you. Kahit ang hirap minsan. I know we’re not the sweet type of couple. We don’t do too much pa-cute or romantic stuff. But this is me being real with you.

You’re not perfect. Alam mo na 'yan. You’ve made mistakes. You’ve hurt me in ways I didn’t expect from someone I trusted that much. Pero even with all that, I still see the parts of you that matter. Yung side mo na tahimik pero sincere. Yung effort mo kahit hindi laging obvious. Yung struggle mong baguhin sarili mo kahit hindi ko hinihingi. I see those. I notice them. And I hold on to them.

Sometimes it hurts how you pull away when I need you the most. How you shut down instead of letting me in. Pero maybe that’s your way of protecting yourself. And maybe that’s why I never gave up. Kasi I know what it feels like to protect yourself from pain. That’s how I know. Mahal kita. Not because you're always good to me, but because I still choose you kahit masakit. That’s real love, right?

But love is not just about holding on. I’ve realized something lately. The true meaning of love is also learning to love yourself. And that’s what I want for you. I want you to stop looking for love in things or people that break you. You keep trying to be okay for everyone else, but I hope one day you’ll do it for yourself too. Because you deserve peace. You deserve healing. And that starts with you.

Thank you for trying. Thank you for staying with me even when we were both tired. Thank you for the small things I don’t always notice. And sorry. Sorry for the days I was cold. Sorry for the times I didn’t understand where you were coming from. I know I have a strong personality, and I know it gets too much sometimes. But please know that everything I say, every time I fight for you, it’s because I care. I really do.

My wish for you today is peace of mind. Real joy. A love that makes you feel safe—not just from me, but from yourself. I hope you find comfort in your own presence. I hope you learn to let go of things that drain you. And I hope you start believing that you are enough, even without needing to prove anything to anyone.

The song I dedicate to you is Those Eyes. Because every time I look at you, especially during the quiet moments, I still see the boy I fell in love with. The boy who hides so much pain but still smiles. The boy who still tries. And I wish, even just once, you could see yourself the way I see you.

Here’s a verse I want to leave you with:

Ecclesiastes 3:11
“He has made everything beautiful in its time.”

Maybe this season is not perfect. Maybe we are not perfect. But we are growing. And that’s enough for now.

I love you. I always will.
Please don’t forget to love yourself too.
Yurie

Happy 20th Birthday, Love

"You’ve made it this far. Keep growing, keep healing."

I’m proud of you. I’m grateful for you. And I’m staying—through every season, every version of you.
